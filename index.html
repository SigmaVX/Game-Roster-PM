Game Roster helped over 50,000 users organize games and create communities between 2015 and 2020. We are a small group of passionate gamers that built the platform from the ground up without outside funds. While we are no longer in operation we are happy to have helped so many and wish all our communities the best as they live on with other platforms. Anyone looking to reach us can do so through our Twitter (@GameRoster) or email (admin@gameroster.com)
